# Quarterly Review

Every 90 days, zoom way out. Deep strategic review and recalibration.

## Process

### 1. Gather All Data

This is comprehensive. Read:
- All `/reviews/monthly/` from the quarter
- All `/reviews/weekly/` from the quarter
- `/metrics/` folders for trends
- All project files (active and archived from this quarter)
- `/reference/goals/[year]-goals.md`
- `/reference/goals/[quarter]-goals.md`
- `/reference/goals/10-year-vision.md`
- Previous quarter's review if exists

### 2. Vision Alignment Check

Start with the 10-year vision:

"Read your 10-year vision. Does it still resonate? Has anything changed?"

Then: "Based on where you are now, are you moving toward that vision or away from it?"

Be honest. If there's drift, name it.

### 4. Major Accomplishments

Look at the full quarter:
- What are you most proud of?
- What moved the needle most?
- What surprised you (positively)?

### 5. Major Failures

Be brutally honest:
- What did you commit to and not do?
- What patterns of failure repeated all quarter?
- What should you have done that you didn't?
- What excuses do you keep making?

"What would you do differently if you could restart this quarter?"

### 6. Key Learnings

"What did you learn about yourself this quarter?"

Categories:
- About your work style
- About your energy management
- About what motivates you
- About what drains you
- About your limits
- About your capabilities

### 7. System Effectiveness Review

Review the Life OS itself:
- What's working well in this system?
- What's not being used?
- What's missing?
- What commands/processes should be added?
- What should be simplified?

### 8. Next Quarter Planning

#### Set Q[X] 2026 Goals

Guide through goal setting:
- Max 3-5 major goals
- Must be specific and measurable
- Must connect to annual goals
- Must have clear success criteria

Format:
```markdown
## Q[X] 2026 Goals

### Goal 1: [Title]
**Target**: [Specific, measurable outcome]
**Why it matters**: [Connection to bigger picture]
**Key milestones**:
- [ ] [Milestone 1] by [date]
- [ ] [Milestone 2] by [date]
**Success criteria**: [How we know it's done]

### Goal 2: [Title]
[Same format]
```

#### Key Initiatives

What are the major initiatives for the quarter?
- Projects to complete
- Habits to establish
- Skills to develop
- Relationships to invest in

#### Potential Obstacles

"What could derail this quarter?"
- Identify risks
- Plan mitigations

### 9. Health Protocol Check

Specifically for the transformation:
- Physical progress (measurements, benchmarks, photos if taken)
- Mental/emotional progress
- Professional progress
- What's working in the protocol?
- What needs adjustment?

### 11. Update Goal Files

- Archive current quarter goals to review file
- Create new `/reference/goals/q[X]-2026-goals.md` with next quarter's goals

### 12. Update Annual Goals (if needed)

If annual goals need adjustment based on Q1 progress, update `/reference/goals/2026-goals.md`

### 13. Reset and Commit

"New quarter starts now. What's your commitment for the first week?"

Get specific. Set up for strong start.

### 14. Optional: Update Vision

If the 10-year vision needs refinement, update `/reference/goals/10-year-vision.md`
